Hello, United Kittens of America!

It's no secret that we live in times that feel remarkably polarized.

There are so many options for the expression of righteous and jusified outrage. Here are a bunch of things you should do:

  • Call your representatives. Here's an easy way to find out who to talk to.
  • Donate money to non-profits that represent your values. Even $5/month makes a difference.
  • Boycott brands and businesses that give money to candidates you oppose. For example, try using your local credit union instead of a big bank.
  • Actively engage in self-care.
What, what? Self care?

Yep, self-care is more important than ever these days. Why?

  1. You can't support others when you're mentally and physically drained. And supporting others is what this is all about.
  2. Self-care activity directly counteracts the negative effects of consuming depressing news all day.
  3. Taking care of yourself reminds you that there are reasons to resist, and important things to fight for.
  4. Self-care improves your mental acuity, aka helps you tune in to what may or may not be BS.
